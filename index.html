High-Performance Powder Metallurgy & Sintered Metal Solutions

Leading Powder Metallurgy Manufacturer: Precision in Every Particle

As a premier Powder Metallurgy (PM) metal parts manufacturer, we specialize in transforming metal powders into high-precision, complex components through the advanced sintering process. Our technology allows us to replicate intricate customer designs with exceptional accuracy, offering a highly cost-effective solution for high-volume production.

By utilizing state-of-the-art sintering techniques, we produce components that exhibit mechanical properties and strength comparable to forged parts, providing our clients with a superior balance of performance and value.

Diverse Material Expertise

At Yeh Sheng, we understand that material selection is critical to engineering success. Our expertise spans a wide range of high-performance materials tailored for demanding industrial environments:

  • • Stainless Steel & Iron: For superior corrosion resistance and structural durability.
  • • Copper Alloys: Ideal for thermal and electrical conductivity.
  • • Soft Magnetic Materials: Essential for modern electronic and electromagnetic applications.

Innovation Beyond Manufacturing

We do more than just manufacture; we are your strategic partner in innovation. At Yeh Sheng, we pride ourselves on "thinking outside the box" to solve complex engineering challenges. From initial prototyping to mass production, our comprehensive OEM and ODM solutions are designed to streamline your supply chain.

By integrating our deep industry experience with cutting-edge metallurgy, we provide tailored metal solutions that meet the rigorous standards of global markets.

Industry Applications

Our powder metallurgy components power critical systems across diverse sectors, delivering reliability where it matters most.

Automotive & Motorcycle Parts

High-strength gears, oil pump rotors, and shock absorber parts designed for durability and performance.

Mechanical Parts

Power Tool Parts

Hardware & Electronics
