Struggling with High Machining Costs or Complex Metal Designs?

Traditional machining wastes material and inflates costs — especially for complex, high-volume parts. Powder Metallurgy (PM) delivers near-net-shape precision with significant cost savings, eliminating the need for expensive secondary machining.

Why Choose Powder Metallurgy?

✓ Near-net-shape production — minimal material waste and machining
✓ Batch-to-batch consistency — repeatable ±0.01mm precision
✓ 97%+ material utilization — drastically reduced scrap rate
✓ Complex geometries — internal features impossible with traditional machining
✓ Scalable for mass production — ideal for 5,000+ unit runs
✓ Self-lubricating options — oil-impregnated bearings and bushings
