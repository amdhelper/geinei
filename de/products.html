Präzisions-Pulvermetallurgieteile für Ihre Bedürfnisse for Your Diverse Needs

Yesheng Pulvermischen Metallurgy has over 40 years of precision manufacturing experience, specializing in the design and production of powder metallurgy parts. Our products achieve tolerances of ±0.01mm and are widely used in automotive, industrial equipment, medical devices, aerospace, and other high-precision fields. We are ISO 9001 certified and provide 100% customization services, from design, material selection, mold making to mass production, offering you the most cost-effective solutions.
