# Project Rules - Yeh Sheng Website

## Applications Page Images

For future updates of the Applications page (`applications.html` and its localized versions), please review and prioritize the following criteria for selecting industry card images:

### Image Selection Priorities

* **Priority 1:** Actual production parts
* **Priority 2:** Part family photographs
* **Priority 3:** Application-related component photos
* **Priority 4:** Manufacturing process photos

### Avoid at All Costs

* Generic stock photos
* Abstract industrial graphics
* AI-generated factory scenes
* Non-authentic engineering imagery
* Generic product collages that do not communicate Yeh Sheng as a manufacturing factory
