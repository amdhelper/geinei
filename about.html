About Us

A Legacy of Precision and Innovation

Company Profile

Founded in 1980, Yeh Sheng Powder Parts Ind. Co. Ltd. has established itself as a premier manufacturer of high-precision powder metallurgy components. With over four decades of experience, we have continuously evolved our technology and processes to meet the rigorous demands of the automotive, medical, and industrial sectors.

Our commitment to quality is evidenced by our ISO 9001:2015 and IATF 16949 certifications. We leverage advanced compaction presses, sintering furnaces, and secondary machining equipment to deliver parts with exceptional dimensional accuracy and mechanical properties.

Our Mission

To provide innovative powder metallurgy solutions that enhance the performance and reliability of our customers' products worldwide.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in precision sintered components, driving advancements in green energy and smart manufacturing.
